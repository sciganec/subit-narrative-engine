## üìù SAMPLE TEXT

```
Da dro bi bar. Ba bar be rab. Bi bar, da dra dar rab.
De dre: "Da bord do." Da rib bord de.
Di dro bu de bi rad. Rad be rab, bi rad ri dib ri rab.
Di dra bri ri rib. Bri be ba rad, rib be ba rad.
Di rde be derb be rab. Di fe be di be re.
Da fe be da bord derb, ri da bord do.
Do bord da ri? Ra, da fe.
Re da ro bord do, da berd. Re da bord do, da derb.
Derb ri berd be rer ro dar. Re da dra derb, da bord dra rab.
Re da dra berd, da bord fe be berd be ro bard.
```

**Translation:**
I go into the house. This house is good. In the house, I see a good person.
He says: "I love you." I also love him.
We go with him into the garden. The garden is good, in the garden there are trees and flowers.
We see birds and stones. Birds are on the garden, stones are on the garden.
We think that life is good. We know that we are one.
I know that I love life, and I love you.
Do you love me? Yes, I know.
If I do not love you, I die. If I love you, I live.
Life and death are two sides of a person. If I see life, I love to see good.
If I see death, I know that death is not the end.
