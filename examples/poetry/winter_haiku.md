## Analysis

This haiku sequence embodies the Philosopher archetype ‚Äî the one who seeks understanding, who questions, who reflects. Each haiku presents a moment of contemplation:

1. **Snow on the book** ‚Äî knowledge frozen, waiting to be read, waiting for spring (understanding)
2. **Mirror** ‚Äî self-reflection, the shock of aging, the question of identity
3. **Night** ‚Äî the vastness of time, the possibility of multiple lives
4. **Wind** ‚Äî questions as elemental forces, not answers
5. **No answers** ‚Äî the philosophical acceptance that questions may be enough

The sequence moves from external observation (snow/book) through self-reflection (mirror) to cosmic scale (night) and finally to acceptance (only snow).
